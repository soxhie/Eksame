Whiskey cocktails

Whiskey Sour

Ingredients:

  • - 5cl Bourbon
  • - 4cl lemon juice
  • - 3cl egg whites
  • -2cl simple syrup
  • -2 dashes of Angustura Bitter

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker. Shake well, then either pour directly into a lowball glass or strain the liquid into a lowball glass filled with cubed ice.

Gold Rush

Ingredients:

  • -5cl Bourbon
  • -3cl lemon juice
  • -2cl honey

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker. Shake well, then double strain into a chilled martini glass.
