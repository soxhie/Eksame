Rum cocktails

Pina Colada

Ingredients:

  • - 5cl light rum
  • - 5cl coconut milk
  • - 2cl simple syrup
  • - A dash of cream to thicken
  • - 1cl lime juice

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker with cubed ice, shake well. Strain into a hurricane glass filled with cubed ice.
