Bitter cocktails

Aperol Spritz

Ingredients:

  • - 5cl Aperol
  • - 4cl cava or cremant
  • - 4cl sheltzer water
  • - orange slice

Instructions:

Pour cava/cremant into a large whine glass, add the sheltzer water and aperol and orange slice.Top off with ice.

Old fashioned

Ingredients:

  • -5cl bourbon
  • -1 sugar cube
  • -2 dashes of Angustura Bitters

Instructions:

Put the sugar cube into a mixing glass, gently muddle, add the Angustura, then add the bourbon. Place 1 large ice cube into the glass and stirr for 1 minute. Strain into a glass with one large icecube, express the oil from an orange peel over the glass, then drop it into the glass.

Negroni

Ingredients

  • -2cl Gin
  • -2cl sweet Vermouth
  • -2cl Campari
  • -1 orange twist

Instructions

Put the ingredients into a mixing glass with ice, stirr untill chilled. Strain into a glass with one large ice cube, add the orange twist.
