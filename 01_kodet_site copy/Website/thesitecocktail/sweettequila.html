Tequila cocktails

Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients:

  • - 5cl Tequila
  • - 3cl orange juice
  • - 3cl grenadine

Instructions:

Pour tequila and orange juice over cubed ice in an highball glass, then top off with grenadine.
