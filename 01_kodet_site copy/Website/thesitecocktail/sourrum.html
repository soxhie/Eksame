Rum cocktails

Daiquiri

Ingredients:

  • - 5cl white Rum
  • - 2,5cl lemon juice
  • -2,5cl simple syrup

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker with cubed ice. Shake well, then double strain into a chilled martini glass.

Mai Tai

Ingredients:

  • -3cl white Rum
  • -2cl dark Rum
  • -1,5cl Cointreau
  • -1,5cl Orgeat syrup
  • -2cl lime juice

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake well, then strain into a highball glass over cubed ice.
