Tequila cocktails

Margarita

Ingredients:

  • - 3cl Tequila
  • - 2cl Cointreau
  • -2cl simple syrup
  • -2cl lime juice
  • -salt

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker over cubed ice. Shake well, then double strain into a martini glass with a salt rim.

Mexican Mule

Ingredients:

  • -5cl Tequila
  • -2cl lime juice
  • -12cl ginger beer
  • -2 dashes of Angustura Bitter

Instructions:

Put all ingredients execpt Angustura into a shaker over cubed ice. Shake well, then strain into a lowball glass, finally add the Angustura.
