Gin cocktails

Singapore Sling

Ingredients:

  • -3cl gin
  • -1cl cherry Heering
  • -1cl Cointreau
  • -1cl Dom Benedictine
  • -1,5 lime juice
  • -1cl grenadine juice
  • -3cl pineapple juice
  • -1 dash of Angustura Bitter

Instructions:

Put alll ingredients execpt grenadine into a shaker.Shake well, then strain into a highball glass. Top off with crushed ice and add the grenadine.

Gimlet

Ingredients:

  • -5cl Gin
  • -5cl lime juice
  • -2cl of simple syrup

Instructions:

Pour all ingredients into a highball glass over cubed ice. If wanted add a salt rim to the glass beforehand.
