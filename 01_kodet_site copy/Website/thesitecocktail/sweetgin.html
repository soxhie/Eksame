Gin cocktails

Bee's Knee's

Ingredients:

  • - 5cl gin
  • - 3cl lemon juice
  • - 3cl honey

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker with cubed ice, shake well. Then double strain into a chilled martini glass.

Aviation

Ingredients:

  • -4cl gin
  • -1cl luxardo maraschino
  • -1cl lemon juice
  • -1cl violet syrup

Instructions:

Put all ingredients into a shaker. Shake well, then double strain into a chilled martini glass and add a maraschino cherry.
